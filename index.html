Ecuador

The smallest of the Andean countries Ecuador, land of four worlds, surprises by the diversity of its landscapes, beaches, the Andes mountain range with its snowy volcanoes and dotted with blue lakes, Amazon jungles and the Galapagos Islands, a volcanic archipelago, historical centers full of churches, monasteries, squares and beautifully restored mansions to walk through its cobblestone streets or along its rivers. It is a way to contemplate its past and present.

Argentina.

Argentina is an immense land and its breathtaking variety of landscapes combine to make Argentina the exciting nation it is today. Its landscapes ranging from its warm and humid minor lands to the steppes, vineyards, and deserts of the northwest, passing through the beautiful capital, Buenos Aires and its extensive suburbs, the fertile land to the archipelago of Tierra del Fuego, behind its historical revolutionary mark and behind the European Tin of Argentina, hides a Latin American nation, colorful, charming, passionate about music, meat and soccer.

Espa単a.

Southern Spain is an infinitely varied seducer, boasting glorious historic cities, glorious monuments and fairytale creations of Moorish architecture with its glorious beaches and enchanting watery marshes. Spain's most extensive region, scenically vast and culturally rich, offers a wide range of attractions, the fiery flamenco, a sizzling gastronomic dinner, marinas and marinas, beautiful villages nestled with the sierras and the sea.

Italia.

Italy is a country that inspires the imagination and surprises with its cities, villages, ancient ruins, extravagant palaces, evocative landscapes full of vineyards that dot the peninsula, a tour of Italy is a walk through history from the lacework of banatic palaces to the refined renaissance of Florence with a variety of cultures and art not seen anywhere else in the world, the land of espresso and pizza and the renaissance of art that inspires from its beautiful cardillera in the north to the cliff-lined coasts of the south.
